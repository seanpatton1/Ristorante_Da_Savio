About Ristorante Da Savio

Authentic Italian, Vegetarian & Vegan Cuisine

At Ristorante Da Savio, we are passionate about delivering authentic Italian flavors with a plant-based twist. From hand-tossed pizzas topped with fresh vegetables to rich and creamy vegan pastas, every dish is crafted with love using the finest plant-based ingredients. Our recipes stay true to Italian tradition while offering a delicious selection for both vegetarians and vegans. We source only the freshest local and organic produce to bring you a truly unique dining experience.

Our journey began with a love for family, tradition, and authentic Italian cooking. Our founder, Savio, grew up in a small village in Italy, learning the secrets of true Italian cooking from his grandmother. Inspired by his passion for plant-based cuisine, Savio decided to reimagine traditional Italian recipes to align with a more sustainable and compassionate approach. Today, we bring those cherished, plant-based recipes to you, ensuring that every meal is both delicious and kind to the planet.

Our Unique Vegetarian & Vegan Experience

Whether you're joining us for a romantic evening or a family gathering, our warm atmosphere and classic Italian hospitality will make your time with us unforgettable. Our menu is entirely vegetarian and vegan, featuring carefully selected wines, wood-fired pizzas, fresh salads, and hearty pasta dishes that will make even the most devoted carnivores reconsider. At Ristorante Da Savio, we offer an authentic Italian experience with a modern, plant-based twist.
